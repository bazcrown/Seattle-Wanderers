About our Team 1 project

Hello and Welcome to our site! We are Team 1 of The Art of Web Design at SeattleU

This site is a group project by Michael, Marc, J Guadalupe, Barbara, and Will. We are making a activities site for things to do in Seattle, Washington.

Our goal for this project is to create a functional website while working on our web design skill set and practicing soft skills for working as a team.

We value accessability and usability in our web design across different devices and platforms. One of our goals is to provide a seamless and intuitive user experience and to accomodate those with disabilities.
