Seattle Coffee Guide

Explore the best coffee shops in Seattle!

Introduction

Welcome to the Seattle Coffee Guide! Whether you're a local or just visiting, Seattle offers a rich coffee culture with numerous excellent coffee shops. Here are some of the best places to get your caffeine fix in the city.

Top Coffee Shops

Starbucks Reserve Roastery & Tasting Room

Address: 1124 Pike St, Seattle, WA 98101

Experience the birthplace of Starbucks in a unique and immersive environment. Enjoy exclusive coffee blends and watch the roasting process live.

Storyville Coffee Company

Address: 94 Pike St #34, Seattle, WA 98101

Located in the heart of Pike Place Market, Storyville offers a cozy atmosphere with expertly crafted coffee and delicious pastries.

Elm Coffee Roasters

Address: 240 2nd Ave S, Seattle, WA 98104

Elm Coffee Roasters is known for its high-quality, freshly roasted beans. The minimalist space provides a perfect environment for enjoying a perfect cup of coffee.

Vivace Espresso Bar

Address: 532 Broadway E, Seattle, WA 98102

Vivace Espresso Bar is famed for its meticulously crafted espresso drinks. It's a must-visit for any coffee aficionado.
